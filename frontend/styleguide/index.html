Yanbal Style Guide

Yanbal Style Guide as the single source of truth for all processes and beliefs within the brand. Like any tool or methodology, it will only work well when used correctly. And "right" can only be determined through a process of constant polishing and practice.

Define

Ensuring a consistent look and feel, while harmonizing multiple products and applications, is demanding and costly. It involves rebuilding them from scratch and adapting them to UX standards. This Style Guide has been created out of the need to address precisely that.
